How to Play
The Basics
Hold the screen to buy. Release to sell and lock in your profit or loss.
Your position uses 2× leverage — gains and losses are both amplified.
A small holding fee ticks up the longer you stay in a trade. Don't overstay.
Breaking news events hit without warning. Crashes drop hard. Pumps spike fast. React fast.
Danger Zone
Down 45%+ on a trade? The screen pulses red — you're in the danger zone.
A red bar fills from the bottom. If it completes (~1.5 sec), your position is auto-liquidated.
Recover above –30% loss and the danger zone resets. Sell fast or ride it out.
Chart Overlays
SMA (blue dotted) — 20-period Simple Moving Average. Price above the line = uptrend; below = downtrend.
WMA (amber dotted) — 14-period Weighted Average. Reacts faster than SMA because recent prices weigh more.
Indicators — tap panel to switch
RSI 14 — Relative Strength Index, 0–100. Above 70 = overbought (likely to drop soon). Below 30 = oversold (bounce incoming). Best time to buy is near 30; best time to sell is near 70.
MACD — Moving Average Convergence Divergence. Blue line crossing above amber = bullish momentum building. Blue crossing below = bearish. The histogram bars show the gap between them — wider bars mean stronger momentum.
MOM 10 — Raw price momentum over 10 ticks. Green = price rising, red = price falling. Momentum turning from red to green is an early entry signal.
